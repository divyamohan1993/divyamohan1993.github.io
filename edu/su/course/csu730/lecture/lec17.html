Sample MCQ for Lecture 17: Comparision Contrast

Q1. Which type of phrase is used to compare two or more things?

  2. Verb phrase

  4. Adverb phrase

Q2. In which type of sentence is a comparison made?

  1. Simple
  2. Complex

Q3. What is the comparative form of the adjective "large"?

Q4. What word is used to indicate similarity between two things being compared?

  2. Better
  3. Best
  4. Gooder

Q9. Which type of verb is used in a comparison?

  1. Transitive verb
  2. Intransitive verb
  3. Linking verb
  4. Action verb

Q10. In which sentence is the comparison and contrast made correctly?

  1. She is not as smart like her sister, but she is more hardworking.
  2. She is not as smart as her sister, but she is more hardworking
  3. She is not as smart as her sister, but more hardworking
